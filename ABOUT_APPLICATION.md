# What is our application?
- A RESTful API-based accounting system built on Laravel, following SAK EMKM (Indonesian SME Accounting Standard) with integrated inventory, manufacturing, and project costing modules.
- Have Killer features specifically to ease their pain points, helping them getting more Project Revenue.
## Target customers?
1. Indonesian SMEs in:
- Electrical panel manufacturing - custom panel builders (to provide multiple alternative BoM (From ABB, Siemes, Scheniders to produce Quotation that will likely win the project)
- Solar EPC contractors - solar installation project companies

## Their pain points?
1. Manual/spreadsheet chaos - tracking jobs, materials, and finances separately
2. No visibility - can't see real profitability per project/work order
3. Inventory guesswork - don't know what materials they have or need (hence MRP)
4. Subcontractor tracking - managing external labor costs and retention
5. Cash flow blindness - AR/AP aging, payment reminders scattered
6. Compliance burden - need SAK EMKM compliant books for tax/bank purposes
